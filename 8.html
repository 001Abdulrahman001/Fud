Chin Chin

Ingridients

  • All-purpose flour
  • sugar
  • butter or margarine
  • milk
  • eggs
  • nutmeg
  • salt
  • vegetable oil for frying

Method

Step One

In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, nutmeg, and salt.

Step Two

Cut in the butter or margarine until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.

Step Three

Beat the eggs and milk together, then add to the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms.

Step Four

Roll out the dough on a floured surface and cut into small squares or strips.

Step Five

Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or skillet over medium heat.

Step Six

Fry the chin chin in batches until golden brown and crispy.

Step Seven

Drain on paper towels and allow to cool before serving.
