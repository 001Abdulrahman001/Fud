Nigerian Yam Porridge (Asaro)

Ingridients

  • Yam
  • palm oil
  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • bell peppers
  • smoked fish or meat (optional)
  • crayfish (dried shrimp)
  • salt

Method

Step One

Peel yam and cut it into cubes.

Step Two

In a pot, heat palm oil and saut√© chopped onions, tomatoes, bell peppers, and scotch bonnet peppers until softened.

Step Three

Add smoked fish or meat (if using) and crayfish, and cook for a few minutes.

Step Four

Add the yam cubes to the pot and enough water to cover them. Season with salt.

Step Five

Cook until the yam is tender and the porridge has thickened to your desired consistency.
