Meat Pie

Ingridients

  • All-purpose flour
  • butter or margarine
  • salt
  • cold water
  • curry powder
  • thyme
  • vegetable oil

Method

Step One

Prepare the dough by mixing flour, salt, and cold butter until crumbly. Gradually add cold water and knead until a smooth dough forms. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.

Step Two

For the filling, saut√© minced meat, onions, carrots, potatoes, and green peas in vegetable oil until cooked through. Season with curry powder, thyme, and salt.

Step Three

Roll out the chilled dough and cut into circles.

Step Four

Place a spoonful of the meat filling on one half of each dough circle, then fold the other half over to enclose the filling.

Step Five

Crimp the edges with a fork to seal and brush with beaten egg for a golden finish.

Step Six

Bake in a preheated oven until golden brown and cooked through.
