Puff Puff

Ingridients

  • All-purpose flour
  • sugar
  • yeast
  • warm water
  • salt
  • vegetable oil for frying

Method

Step One

Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.

Step Two

In a large bowl, mix flour and salt, then add the yeast mixture and mix until smooth.

Step Three

Cover the bowl with a cloth and let the batter rise for about 1 hour.

Step Four

Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or skillet over medium heat.

Step Five

Scoop spoonfuls of the batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and cooked through.

Step Six

Drain on paper towels and serve warm.
