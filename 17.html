Ogbono Soup

Ingridients

  • Ground ogbono seeds
  • assorted meats (beef, chicken, fish, etc.)
  • palm oil
  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • bell peppers
  • scotch bonnet peppers
  • spinach or bitter leaf
  • crayfish (dried shrimp)
  • salt

Method

Step One

Heat palm oil in a pot and saut√© chopped onions until translucent.

Step Two

Add blended tomatoes, bell peppers, and scotch bonnet peppers. Cook until the oil separates.

Step Three

Stir in ground ogbono seeds and cook for a few minutes.

Step Four

Pour in stock or water and bring to a simmer.

Step Five

Add assorted meats and cook until tender.

Step Six

Add crayfish and cook for another 5 minutes.

Step Seven

Stir in chopped spinach or bitter leaf and simmer until wilted.

Step Eight

Season with salt to taste and serve hot with fufu or pounded yam.
