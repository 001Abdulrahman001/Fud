Bitterleaf Soup (Ofe Onugbu)

Ingridients

  • Bitterleaf
  • assorted meats
  • palm oil
  • onions
  • crayfish
  • stock cubes
  • salt

Method

Step One

Boil bitterleaf to remove bitterness, drain, and set aside.

Step Two

Cook assorted meats with chopped onions until tender.

Step Three

Add palm oil, crayfish, stock cubes, and water. Simmer.

Step Four

Stir in bitterleaf and simmer until flavors meld.

Step Five

Season with salt and serve hot with fufu or pounded yam.
