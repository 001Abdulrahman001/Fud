Akara (Bean Cakes)

Ingridients

  • Black-eyed peas
  • onions
  • scotch bonnet peppers (or bell peppers)
  • salt
  • vegetable oil for frying

Method

Step One

Soak black-eyed peas in water overnight, then peel off the skins.

Step Two

In a blender or food processor, blend the peeled black-eyed peas with onions and peppers until smooth.

Step Three

Transfer the mixture into a bowl, add salt, and stir well.

Step Four

Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or skillet over medium heat.

Step Five

Scoop spoonfuls of the bean batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.

Step Six

Remove from oil and drain on paper towels. Serve hot with pap (fermented cornmeal pudding) or bread.
