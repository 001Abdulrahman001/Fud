Kuli-Kuli

Ingridients

  • Groundnuts (peanuts)
  • groundnut oil
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • salt
  • cayenne pepper (optional)

Method

Step One

Grind the groundnuts into a fine powder using a food processor or mortar and pestle.

Step Two

Add grated ginger, minced garlic, salt, and cayenne pepper to the groundnut powder and mix well.

Step Three

Gradually add groundnut oil to the mixture while kneading until it forms a smooth dough.

Step Four

Shape the dough into small balls or flatten into discs.

Step Five

Deep fry the kuli-kuli in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.

Step Six

Drain on paper towels and let them cool before serving.
