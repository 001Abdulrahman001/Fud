Plantain Chips

Ingridients

  • Unripe plantains
  • salt
  • salt
  • vegetable oil for frying

Method

Step One

Peel the plantains and slice them thinly using a knife or mandoline.

Step Two

Soak the plantain slices in salted water for a few minutes, then drain and pat dry with paper towels.

Step Three

Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or skillet over medium heat.

Step Four

Fry the plantain slices in batches until golden brown and crispy.

Step Five

Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.

Step Six

Sprinkle with additional salt if desired and allow to cool before serving.
