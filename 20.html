Method

Step One

Blend tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, and scotch bonnet peppers into a smooth paste.

Step Two

Heat vegetable oil in a pot, saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.

Step Three

Add minced garlic and ginger, cook until fragrant.

Step Four

Stir in tomato paste, curry powder, thyme, and bay leaves, cook for a few minutes.

Step Five

Add the blended pepper mixture, chicken or beef stock, and salt. Simmer until the sauce thickens.

Step Six

Rinse rice and add to the pot, stirring to coat with the sauce.

Step Seven

Add assorted vegetables and cooked chicken or beef if desired.

Step Eight

Cover and simmer until the rice is cooked through.
