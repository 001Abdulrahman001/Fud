Akamu (Ogi/Pap)

Ingridients

  • Cornmeal or millet flour
  • sugar
  • milk (optional)
  • evaporated milk (optional)
  • Water

Method

Step One

Mix cornmeal or millet flour with water in a pot, stirring to avoid lumps.

Step Two

Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened.

Step Three

Add sugar to taste if desired.

Step Four

Serve hot or cold, optionally with milk or evaporated milk.
