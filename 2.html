Avocado Toast

Ingridients

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, poached egg

Method

Step One

Toast the slices of whole grain bread until golden brown.

Step Two

Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

Step Three

Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth and spreadable.

Step Four

Season the mashed avocado with salt and pepper to taste.

Step Five

Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto the toasted bread slices

Step Six

Add optional toppings such as sliced cherry tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, or a poached egg on top.

Step Seven

Serve immediately and enjoy!
