Pepper Soup

Ingridients

  • Assorted meats
  • onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • scotch bonnet peppers
  • stock cubes
  • scent leaves
  • salt

Method

Step One

Boil meats with chopped onions, garlic, and ginger until tender.

Step Two

Add chopped scotch bonnet peppers, stock cubes, and water. Simmer.

Step Three

Season with salt and simmer until flavors meld.

Step Four

Stir in scent leaves and serve hot as a soup or with boiled yam.
