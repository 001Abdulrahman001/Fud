Method

Step One

Peel ripe plantains and cut them into diagonal slices.

Step Two

Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat and fry the plantain slices until golden brown on both sides. Remove and drain on paper towels.

Step Three

In the same skillet, saut√© chopped onions, tomatoes, bell peppers, and scotch bonnet peppers until softened.

Step Four

Crack eggs into the skillet and scramble until fully cooked. Season with salt and black pepper.

Step Five

Serve the fried plantains and eggs together for a delicious and satisfying breakfast.
