Method

Step One

Peel yam and cut into cubes. Boil in salted water until tender, then drain and set aside.

Step Two

In a skillet, heat vegetable oil and saut√© chopped onions, tomatoes, bell peppers, scotch bonnet peppers, and garlic until softened.

Step Three

Crack eggs into the skillet and scramble until fully cooked.

Step Four

Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Step Five

Serve the egg sauce hot over boiled yam slices.
